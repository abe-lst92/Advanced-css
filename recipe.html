Family Recipe

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Cheese.
  • White Onion.
  • Cooking Oil(any oil).
  • Garlic
  • Dried Chile(Pepper) Guajillos

Ingredients

Step 1

Put the dried chiles(peppers) into a saucepan with water, after that put them into the kitchen stove heat for like 15 minutes.

Step 2

After the chiles are ready put them into the blender with the garlic clove and 1/2 cup of water and start the blender until the chiles become completely like a sauce. When the chile sauce is ready put it into a a bowl or the same saucepan so you can have it ready to prepare you enchiladas.

Step 3

Now in another skillet add the oil and put it into heat and put the corn tortilla into the hot oil. Fried the tortilla both ways but not to fried just put it into the hot oil for a couple seconds and move the tortilla where you have the chile sauce.

Step 4

After Moving the fried tortilla into the chile sauce make sure to fill the tortilla both sides with the chile sauce. After having the tortilla with the hot chile sauce put the tortilla into a plate and fill the tortilla with the cheese and you can create as much enchiladas as you want. You can add rice to the enchiladas and Enjoy!!!
